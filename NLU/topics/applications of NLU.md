**Sentiment analysis**
A sentiment is a thought, view or attitude mainly based on emotion instead of reason.
Analysis is the computational treatments for discovering opinions/attitudes expressed in text (e.g. positive/negative)
Can be richer categories like joy/sadness/anger etc as well
A opinion is a quintuple (o,a,so,h,t)
- o is an object or target entity e.g. product or topic
- a is the aspect or attribute of an object e.g. weight, battery
- so is the sentiment orientation of the opinion about an object or aspect
- h is an opinion holder
- t is the timestamp when the opinion was expressed
- e.g. (new car, cost, high, tom, 4th june 12:52)
Explicit vs implicit: "my mood is really bad" vs "the new bike fell apart within 2 days"
Sarcasm - sharp remark conveying opposite of literal meaning e.g. "Exactly what i wanted thanks a lot"
