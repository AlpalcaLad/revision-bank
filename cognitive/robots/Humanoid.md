Has an anthropomorphic body plan and actuators with human-like senses
- cameras for vision
- mics for audio perception
Can be electronic machines, plastic toy or human-like (android)

**Androids**
Intentionally designed with the goal of being human-like, indistinguishable from humans in its external appearance and behaviour. Currently in uncanny valley, not quite human-like something feels a bit off.

**Softbank Pepper**
Released in 2014 as a social robot. Designed to interact with humans, read emotions and assist in service, education and healthcare roles. Plastic with wheels although with a vaguely human structure. Had a tablet camera and sensors

**iCub**
Modelled after a 3-4 year child with 53 degrees of freedom by the Italian Institute of Technology. Advanced hands and sensorised skin, used for testing developmental robotics and learning.
